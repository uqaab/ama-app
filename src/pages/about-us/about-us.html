About

Sawal is a free app baked under shelter of Uqaab - a non profit organization, founded by Shahzad Nawaz, in May 2018.


It provides very easy interface for questions and answers, can be useful for Seminars, Meetups, Mehfil / Bayan, or any kind of crowd-facing event.


They key qualities of Sawal App:

  1. Easy interface to ask questions one-to-one to Admin, and see Admin replies only.
  2. No login and passwords required. Your Phone is your identity.
  3. No sim / phone numbers required or exposed to other channel crowd, not to Admins even
  4. Prevent irrelevant questions. Only approved questions are broadcasted to channel crowd.
  5. Vote up questions you like most, so that Admin can answer most popular questions first.
  6. Smart copy - copy and share any question with all it's replies, and paste/share it to Whatsapp, Facebook, anywhere.
  7. No Personal messages or any other sort of spams.
  8. Of course, all this absolutely FREE, without any Ads.
